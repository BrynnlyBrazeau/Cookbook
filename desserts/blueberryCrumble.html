Blueberry Crumble

My equivalent of birthday cake

time

35 mins

carbs

90

servings

6

instructions (crust)

Preheat oven to 325F Line baking dish with parchment paper Mix almond flour, baking soda, and salt In a seperate bowl mix coconut oil, honey, and coconut flour Stir in almond flour Scoop into baking dish and press it down evenly to form bottom crust Bake for 5 minutes Pour in blueberry preserve

ingredients

Blueberry preserve

1 1/2 cup almond flour

1/4 cup coconut oil

1 tbsp honey

1 tbsp coconut flour

1/2 tsp baking soda

1/2 tsp salt

instructions (topping)

Mix coconut oil and honey Stir in almond flour and coconut flakes Scoop this mixture across the top of the crust/preserve Sprinkle in the crushed almonds Bake for 20 minutes

ingredients

3/4 cup almond flour

1/2 cup coconut flakes

1/4 cup crushed almonds

3 tbsp melted coconut oil

1 tsp honey
