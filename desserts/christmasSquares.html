Christmas Squares

Work in progress

time

20 mins

carbs

90

servings

6

instructions

Blend nuts and scoop into mixing bowl Repeat with other dry ingredients, adding to the same bowl Juice lemon Add lemon juice and mix thoroughly Add maple syrup and mix until consistent Scoop into container and divide into 6 pieces

ingredients

1 lemon

1/2 cup macadamia nuts

1/2 cup coconut flakes

1/4 pumpkin seeds

1/4 cup goji berries

5 tbsp maple syrup

1 tsp pumpkin pie spice
