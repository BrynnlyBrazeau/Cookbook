Amaranth Crackers

Work in progress

time

30 mins

carbs

100

servings

24

instructions

Preheat oven to 355F Mix all ingredients in food processor Scoop onto parchment paper Flatten into 5mm sheet Use fork to prick dough Bake for 12-15 minutes

ingredients

1 cup amaranth flour

3 tbsp olive oil

3 tbsp water

1 tbsp rosemary

1/2 tsp lemon juice

1/2 tsp salt

1/8 tsp baking soda

1 garlic clove

6 leaves thymes
