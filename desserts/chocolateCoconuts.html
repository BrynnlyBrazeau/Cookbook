Chocolate Coconuts

Coconut macaroons with cocoa

time

30 mins

carbs

90

servings

12

instructions

Mash banana in mixing bowl Stir in coconut oil and maple syrup Add cocoa powder, shredded coconut, and salt Use a spoon to scoop chunks onto parchment paper Place in freezer for 20 minutes

ingredients

1 banana

1/2 cup shredded coconut

1/4 cup maple syrup

1/4 cup melted coconut oil

6 tbsp cocoa powder

salt
