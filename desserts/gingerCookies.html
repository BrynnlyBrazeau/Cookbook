Ginger Cookies

Cookies perfect for autumn

time

15 mins

carbs

60

servings

12

instructions

Preheat oven to 350 F Mix all ingredients in a bowl until smooth Line a large baking sheet with parchment paper and scoop out chunks of dough Press down dough into circles Bake in the oven for about 12 minutes

ingredients

1 egg

1/2 cup almond butter

2 tbsp maple syrup

2 tsp pumpkin spice

1/16 tsp salt
