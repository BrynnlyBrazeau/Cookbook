Zucchini Fritters

Refreshing veggie patties

time

30 mins

carbs

N/A

servings

12

instructions

Dry grated zucchini and put it in a large bowl Heat oil to medium heat in a large pan Cook the onion until soft and add zucchini Mix parsley, salt, almond flour, coconut flour, and egg to the zucchini Add clumps to pan and press them flat Fry until both sides are brown

ingredients

1 egg

3 cups grated zucchini

1 cup almond flour

2/3 cup diced onion

6 tbsp parsley

2 tbsp olive oil

2 tbsp coconut flour

3/4 tsp salt
