Almond Waffles

Simple egg-based waffles

time

15 mins

carbs

N/A

servings

5

instructions

Mix ingredients in small bowl Add 4 tablespoons of batter to waffle maker Cook until golden brown Repeat last two steps until there is no remaing batter

ingredients

2 eggs

1/2 cup almond flour

1/2 tsp lemon juice

1/8 tsp baking soda
