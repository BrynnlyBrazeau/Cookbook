Maddie's Cucumbers

An inventive approach to cucumber slices

time

10 mins

carbs

N/A

servings

2

instructions

Slice cucumber into inch-wide slices Steam cucumbers Finely chop tomato and onion Combine the cucumber, tomato, and onion Add salt and cinnamon

ingredients

1 cucumber

1 medium tomato

1 green onion

1 tsp cinnamon

Salt
